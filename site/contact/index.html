What a local visibility audit looks at

A local visibility audit is a practical review of the things that most directly affect whether nearby homeowners can find your business, trust what they see, and take the next step. Instead of vague marketing talk, it looks at the real visibility and conversion points that shape lead flow.

We review how your website presents your services, whether your Google Business Profile supports local map visibility, how strong your trust signals are, and where friction may be costing you calls or quote requests. The goal is to identify the clearest opportunities to improve visibility and conversion without guessing.

  • Website clarity, calls to action, and conversion flow
  • Google Business Profile strength and local market positioning
