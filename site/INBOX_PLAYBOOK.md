## Message triage

### Reply
Use for:
- real leads
- quote requests
- local SEO / website / ads inquiries
- Google Business Profile help
- serious partnership inquiries
- existing client communication

### Ignore / archive
Use for:
- spam
- phishing
- cold SEO pitches
- generic web design sales
- backlink / guest post junk
- fake invoices
- random SaaS spam
- irrelevant vendor outreach

### Escalate before replying
Use for:
- legal issues
- billing/payment disputes
- angry clients
- contracts
- media/PR requests
- serious partnerships
- anything sensitive or unclear

---

## Reply tone
- direct
- professional
- friendly
- human
- brief
- move toward next step

Avoid:
- robotic wording
- long walls of text
- overpromising
- committing to pricing/terms without review

## Qualification checklist
For each real inquiry, try to identify:

### Business
- name
- service area
- business type

### Need
- website
- local SEO
- GBP
- paid ads
- reviews/reputation
- lead gen
- follow-up systems

### Urgency
- immediate
- this month
- researching
- unclear

### Quality
Strong lead signs:
- real business
- clear need
- real context
- provides site/GBP
- asks about next step

Weak signs:
- vague
- generic
- spammy tone
- no real context
- obviously mass outreach
